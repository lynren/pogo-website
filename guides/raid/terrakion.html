TERRAKION RAID GUIDE
Guides

Terrakion is a legendary rock and fighting type Pokémon with a max CP of 3698. It has base stats of 260 attack, 192 defense, and 209 stamina. Terrakion is vulnerable to Fairy, Fighting, Grass, Ground, Psychic, Steel, and Water type moves. Terrakion is boosted in Partly Cloudy and Cloudy weather. Terrakion's best moves are Smack Down and Rock Slide (14.99 DPS).

The best Terrakion counters are Psystrike Mewtwo and Meteor Mash Metagross. If you do not have Legendaries or Community Day Metagross to fight with, Machamp can also work well. Terrakion is a Tier 5 raid boss with 48457 CP. Terrakion can be caught in the following CP ranges:

  • 2026 - 2113 (Unboosted Level 20)
  • 2553 - 2641 (Boosted Level 25
