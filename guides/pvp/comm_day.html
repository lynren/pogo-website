COMMUNITY DAY 2019
Guides

Pokémon Go Community Day 2019 has just been announced recently, catching the attention of players around the world. What makes December's Community Different from others is this time, players will be able to evolve any 2018 or 2019 Community Day Pokémon for their exclusive moves. From Meteor Mash Metagross to Outrage Salamence, if you missed out on these overpowered moves, then December's Community Day is your second chance to get them. To capitalize on this opportunity, start hunting 100IV Community Day Pokémon today!

Exclusive Move Priority List

These are the Pokémon which you should be prioritizing evolution during December Community Day.

For Raids and Gym battles:

  • Metagross (Meteor Mash)
  • Venusaur (Frenzy Plant)
  • Tyranitar (Smack Down)
  • Swampert (Hydro Cannon)

For PvP:

  • Swampert (Hydro Cannon)
  • Umbreon (Last Resort)
  • Venusaur (Frenzy Plant)
