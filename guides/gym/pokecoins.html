POKÉCOINS FROM GYMS
Guides

PokéCoins are a type of currency in Pokémon Go. The coins are used to purchase almost all of the Items in the Shop, with the exclusion of PokéCoins themselves. 100 PokéCoins can be purchased for 1 USD. The more coins you buy at a time, the higher a discount you get per coin. 100 USD will get you 14 500 PokéCoins. To play Pokémon Go at a higher level, a player must use PokéCoins. This is because a lot of quality of life upgrades are only available throught the Item Shop.

Getting Free PokéCoins

The only free way of obtaining PokéCoins is through Gym defense. The way it works is, for every 10 minutes of Gym defense, a player can receive 1 PokéCoin. The minimum defense time to receive any coins is 20 minutes. The initial 10 minute interval is not counted to prevent win trading. The following table outlines some constraints on Gym-Coin model:

Feature Value
Daily Cap 50 PokéCoins
Weekly Cap 350 PokéCoins
Gym Cap 20 Gyms
Collected when Defender returns
Coin Gain 1 PokéCoin per 10 minutes

In summary, if you want free PokéCoins, you must work together with your team to take over Gyms and defend them. Gym defense is always easier with good Gym defenders, so checkout the Best Gym Defenders article for some tips!
