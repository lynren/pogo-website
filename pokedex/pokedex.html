POKÉDEX

The Pokédex is one of the main elements of the game. It's a compendium of information about every Pokémon already caught and seen by player. It becomes more complete as trainers own and encounter new Pokémon.

Excluding Meltan and Melmetal, the Pokémon currently available in Pokémon Go come from the Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, and Unova regions. Not all Pokémon from these regions are available, but with most updates and special events, Niantic implements more Pokémon into the game. Some Pokémon are only available through special events or challenges. These events may only last for a limited time, so make sure to keep an eye on our news section for updates! Furthurmore, you can subscribe to our newsletter to always be up to date with the latest Pokémon Go information.
