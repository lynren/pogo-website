NESTING POKÉMON
Pokédex

Pokémon nests are areas in specific geographical locations where an increased number of the same species of Pokémon appear for a short duration of time. Nest migrations are regular events where a Pokémon nest changes to another location. A migration occurs every Thursday between 0:00 UTC and 0:30 UTC. The 105th migration is scheduled to occur on December 12, 2019. Although nests change according to this schedule, migration also occurs when a new batch of Pokémon are released. When nests migrate, the set of nest locations does not change, only the species nesting at a given nest location will change.
