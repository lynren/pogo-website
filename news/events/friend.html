FRIEND FEST
News

Celebrate friendship during Friend Fest 2019! This in-game event features 1/2 stardust trade cost, two special trades per day, attack boost when raiding with friends, increased spawns of "family themed" Pokémon in the wild, and a 25% damage boost when battling with friends. This event starts on November 27th and will end on December 2nd.

Increased Spawns

The following Pokémon will have an increased spawn rate during the event:

  • Nidoran F
  • Nidoran M
  • Doduo
  • Magnemite
  • Diglett
  • Combee
  • Exeggcute

In addition to the increased spawns, this event marks the implementation of Shiny Nidoran.
