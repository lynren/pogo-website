STORAGE INCREASE
News

The recent update brought something many players were hoping for: storage upgrades! The maximum storage for both Pokémon and Items has increased. Maximum storage capacity for Pokémon has been increased from 2500 to 3000 and the maximum storage capacity for Items has been increased from 2000 to 2500. Both storages can be upgraded for 200 PokéCoins each. Each 200 PokéCoin upgrade will get you 50 more slots of capacity.
